The exact phrase does not indeed seem to be attested in Vedic. Other  
obviously related phrases do however occur very commonly. A GREP  
search bhuut.*bhavi in my collection of (mainly Vedic) e-texts  
yielded 417 hits.

Here is a selection from the beginning of the alphabet:

AB 5.30 	 	yad bhuuta.m bhavi.syac caapi sarvam /

Aap;SS
(20.11.8) bhuuta.m bhavya.m bhavi.syad iti paryaaptii.h
(20.12.9) bhuutaaya svaahaa bhavi.syate svaaheti bhuutaabhavyau homau

AV;S (with parallels in AVP)

vive;saa ;s;aciibhi.h ||20|| {12}
