Out of so many (undeservedly) neglected works, I should like to recommend
Bhaskarakantha's (18th century) independent (.i. e. non-commentatorial)
composition "CittaanubodhaZaastra" for a closer study - just if you are after a
witty mind and skilled writer with autonomous ideas. No risk of entering the
ever boring Advaitavedanta mainstream scholasticism.

Best, WS
