Since the topic has come up, I might mention that I'm thinking about
something like "GRETIL Books", where everyone can contribute whatever
scans/e-books they are prepared to share with others. These e-books would be
catalogued as copies of the paper editions, and would be searchable in a
separate OPAC, for which all the bibliographical data of our main catalogue
would be available, plus a link for download.
 
If there is any interest in this plan, an a serious prospect of cooperation,
I'm ready!
