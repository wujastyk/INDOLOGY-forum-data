Indian sources is:

Eichinger Ferro-Luzzi, Gabriella, 1987. The self-milking cow and the
bleeding lingam: Criss-cross of motifs in Indian temple legends. Wiesbaden:
Otto Harrassowitz. xxiv, 254 pp., ill.
