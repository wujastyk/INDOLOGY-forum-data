The India Centre, FLAME University is delighted to share that we will be
hosting our 20th India Series Webinar. The speaker for this month is *Prof.

Can they Reform?* He is the author or co-author of seven other books and

Century Roundtable. He will be speaking on *How International Democracy
Ratings Misrepresent India?*
