* Kenneth Zysk <zysk at HUM.KU.DK> [2012-03-21 15:00]:
> I have tried to obtain a free, downloadable nagari font that will allow me to
> write the word preman in nagari.  Can anyone help me?

You might want to try one of the following free fonts:

Nakula: http://ftp.de.debian.org/debian/pool/main/f/fonts-nakula/fonts-nakula_1.0.orig.tar.gz

Sahadeva: http://ftp.de.debian.org/debian/pool/main/f/fonts-sahadeva/fonts-sahadeva_1.0.orig.tar.gz

(They are described here: http://bombay.indology.info/software/fonts/devanagari/index.html)

Chandas or Siddhanta, both accessible from here: http://www.sanskritweb.net/cakram/

Lohit: https://fedorahosted.org/releases/l/o/lohit/lohit-devanagari-ttf-2.5.1.tar.gz

Hope this helps,
