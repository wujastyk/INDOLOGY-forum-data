Weekend of 8th/9th January 2005
CLOSURE of Computing Facilities
-------------------------------

UCL Information Systems, which hosts the INDOLOGY website, is planning a
weekend outage of computing and networking services in order to undertake
remedial electrical work.

This outage will run from 17.00hrs on Friday 7th January until 19:00hrs on
Sunday 9th January.  During this time, the INDOLOGY website will be down,
or at best only intermittently available.
