5 Vaikara~nja

  intermixture of three types of darviikara etc. lead to:

  maakuli

  snigharaaji

and these intermix to give seven more:

  divyelaka
  rodhrapu.spaka
  raajicitraka
  po.tagala
  pu.spaabhikiir.na
  darbapu.spa
  vellitaka

This should add up to 80 snakes, all poisonous.

The symptoms of poisoning by snakes in the groups are described in quite a
lot of detail, which could be matched with more recent clinical and field
descriptions.
