> "TAXILA ACADEMY"

> SUMMER STUDIES IN INDIA, JUNE-JULY '99.
>
> TAXILA Academy is a summer program in Indian Studies set in Bangalore,
> India. It is designed for college students, young professionals, and
> other adults interested in learning about India. The program
> accommodates up to 30 students from around the world. In 1999, TAXILA
> runs for about 6 weeks, from the 18th of June to the 26th of July.
> Students at TAXILA Academy select two courses each, from a total of
> six courses offered in the Humanities and Social Sciences. Coursework
> is supplemented with special lectures, workshops, movie screenings,
> and excursions during weekends. Classroom and field experiences
> complement one another to enable focussed discussions on a
> representative range of subjects concerning India.
