Dear list members,

While the utilities like grep are good for languages with simple
morphology, for Sanskrit, the regular expressions would result either in
under-generation or over-generation, if one would like to search with a
pr?tipadikam/dh?tu.

In order to facilitate such search, we have developed a search engine
Gave?ik?, available at http://sanskrit.uohyd.ac.in:8082/searchengine

This search engine combines the morphology engine with the search engine
making it powerful.
