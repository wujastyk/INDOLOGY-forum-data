    Maitraaya.niiya Upani.sad :-)
    belonging to Krishna Yajurveda possessing a
    Samhita of 4 Kandas of 54 Prapatakas:

1  Robert Ernest Hume,
    Thirteen Principal Upanishads,
    Oxford University Press, 2nd Edn, 1993
    pages 412 to 458

2  S Radhakrishnan,
    The Principal Upanishads,
    Harper Collins Publishers / Indus, 3rd Impression, 1996
    pages 793 to 860

3   Paul Deussen (Translated to English by
    V M Bedekar & GB Palsuke)
     Motilal Banarasidaas, 1990
     Vol 1, pages 327 to 386

4   E B Cowell
     The Maithri Or Maithrayaniya Upanishad
      English translation with the commentary
      of Ramathirtha,
      Watts, London, 1870, 291 pages
      (Bibilotheca Indica)

5   Vidyarnava, Srisa Chandra & Mohanlal Sandal,
     Maithri Upanioshad, the
     with notes & commentary,
     Panini Office, Allahabad, 1926, 155 pages
     (Sacred Books of Hindus, Vol 30, part 2)
