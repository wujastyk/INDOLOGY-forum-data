This exact point was discussed in the Prad?pa and clarified further in the
Uddyota.   One might think, N?ge?a said, that the word mleccha would
usually refer to a person or a place; how could it refer to a bad word?
But in this case it is a term of criticism.  "A bad word (apa?abda) is
really despicable (mleccha)."
