Allen, British network systems reverse the sequence of domain names in
email addresses. I believe the way to send mail to the Indian
Institute is: indinst at vax.ox.ac.uk

(Note that this is very similar to the reversal of INDOLOGY's address.
British mailers send to INDOLOGY at uk.ac.liverpool  but we send to
INDOLOGY at liverpool.ac.uk).
