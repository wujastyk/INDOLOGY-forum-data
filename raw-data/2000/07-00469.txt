An earlier one:

yA pUrvakarmaprabhavasya dhAtrI
     dhAtrA lalATe likhitA pras'astiH
tAM s'Astram etat prakaTaM vidhatte
     dIpo yathA vastughane 'ndhakAre.

MInarAja, VRddhayavanajAtaka I.3 (c. 4th century CE)
