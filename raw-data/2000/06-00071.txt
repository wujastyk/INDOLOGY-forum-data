Shri Shrish Rao put a question., "whether Madhva attain samaadhi in 1317 or
in 1386?"
Madhvacarya, according to the tradition, became 'antardhAna'. In 1386 it was
Madhavacarya alias VidyAraNya becaame "paripUrNa" i.e. united with God.
This is all I know from my research work.
