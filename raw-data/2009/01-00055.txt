> Jews. The large majority of Hindus in the Netherlands are
> migrants from
> Surinam, a former Dutch colony on the Northern coast of
> Brazil. These Hindus
> (in Dutch referred to as Hindostanis) actually came to
> Surinam as bonded
> contract-labourers from the United Provinces mainly,
> between roughly 1870 to
> 1919. Among these labourers about 80 percent was Hindu, and
> the rest were
> Muslims, with a few Sikhs in between. In the seventies of
> the twentieth
> century many of these Surinamese Hindustanis migrated to
> the Netherlands.
> Hence we find many people in the Netherlands of Indian
> origin, not from
> Bali. But as we did not have any censuses taken in the
> Netherlands since the
> early eighties, exact numbers cannot be given.
