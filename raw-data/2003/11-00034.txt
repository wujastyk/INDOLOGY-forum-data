It is my pleasure to pass along the following information regarding
the publication of a fine piece of work, the E-Text
Abhidarma-samuccaya and Bhasya.   The following website

http://www.shiga-med.ac.jp/public/yugagyo/

contains a Skt-Tib and a Chinese edition of this text in pdf format,
available for free download. These materials were prepared by a team
headed by Prof Osamu HAYASHIMA, to whom all scholars interested in
this material should be very grateful.
