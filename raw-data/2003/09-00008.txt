The University of Pennsylvania is looking for an Outreach Coordinator for
their South Asia Center. A coordinator would handle events within the Penn
community, as well as within Philadelphia at large, sponsored by the South
Asia Center. These events would include artistic performances, public
lectures, visits from prestigious scholars, talks at area schools,
refresher courses for area K-12 teachers, and so on. The Outreach
Coordinator would make contacts with the Penn and Philadelphia communities;
visit local schools and businesses to publicize and educate; organize
various events at Penn; and create publications for the Center, such as our
Newsletter, website, and periodic mailings.
