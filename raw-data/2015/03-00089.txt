*Brief introduction* - Upasargama??anam of Ma??anamantr? is one of the
oldest and one of the most comprehensive treatise dealing with Sanskrit
prefixes. It was authored by Mandana, who was prime minister of Malwa. The
work dates back to 1435-46 A.D. It is the finest specimen of subsidiary
texts in the grammatical system of Vopadeva. It uses Kavikalpadruma
dhatupatha as its base. It contains more than 2300 meanings of different
upasarga-verb combinations. This book also contains a scholary introduction
by the editor.
