It's a few years back that I used Windows, but the software Keyman
(Tavultesoft) is quite helpful for creating customized keyboard input.

There is a bundle of Andrew Glass's Gandhari Unicode font together with
the Keyman Desktop software:
http://www.tavultesoft.com/keyman/downloads/keyboards/details.php?KeyboardID=398

Hope this helps, Birgit Kellner
