Chess in India
The century old but still important works of van der Linde on the history of
chess have much on Indian chess literature.  L. was helped in his
researches L. by Albrecht Weber.  I append a list of various versions on L's
major works, including microfilmed and reprint eds.  Again, these are OCLC
FirstSearch records, not Library of Congress records.
