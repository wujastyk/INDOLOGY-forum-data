Savitri is available from Hyperion, CD CDA66099. The CD contains Savitri and
a work called Dream-City, a song cycle arranged and orchestrated by Colin
Mathews.

Choral Hymns from the Rig Veda, group 3 op 26 no. 3, is available from
Hyperion, CD CDA66175 together with works by Benjamin Britten and Arthur Bliss.
