IndoTimes Roman and copyright
Please send queries about fonts, copyright, etc. to one of the usenet
discussion groups about fonts.  E.g., comp.fonts
Fonts and their designs are normally protected by copyright.  So they
can't be copied without permission.  This is usually provided in the
accompanying licence, but will normally be limited in some way.
