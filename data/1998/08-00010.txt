>  Would Xuan Zang (Hsuan Tsang) around 640 A.D.write Buddha as
>  Pu-ta or Po-ta??

By the seventh century in China Buddha was commonly written as the
character fo (BUT by the old pronunciation; still Butsu in Japanese
pronunciation). Xuanzang used that character.
