[INDOLOGY] Axel Michaels receives Lautenschlaeger Research Prize
Dear Colleagues,
it is my great pleasure to inform you that Axel Michaels receives the 
Lautenschlaeger Research Prize 2015, the most highly endowed research 
prize from a private sponsor in Germany. The ceremony will be held on 
4th December at Heidelberg University.
For more information please see the following link:
http://www.asia-europe.uni-heidelberg.de/en/newsevents/news/detail/m/axel-michaels-receives-lautenschlaeger-research-prize.html
(Please note the link to Axel Michaels' recently published book "Homo 
Ritualis. Hindu Ritual and Its Significance for Ritual Theory. New 
York/Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2016. - 400 p. ($ 39.95)")
