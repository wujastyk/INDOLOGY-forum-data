[INDOLOGY] 444 Marathi books available for free downloading
The Maharashtra Rajya Sahitya & Samskriti Mandal has now made 444 of its
Marathi book publications available for free download:
https://msblc.maharashtra.gov.in/download
For those who read Marathi, this is a treasure of rare and out-of-print
books.  In the past, I had to struggle to find many of these books in
bookstores in Pune.  Now they are a click away.  Hope you find something of
value in this collection.
