    If you like to use Unicode-letters for all Windows-apps incl.
      Explorer, Firefox etc.:

    Write a small exe-file with the (free) AutoHotkey-app and
      copy this exe-file into the Autostart-folder of Windows.

    It is really very easy: You just write a list assigning
      unicode-letters to any keyshortcut you like.
