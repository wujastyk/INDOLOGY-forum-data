My index in Roots of Ayurveda says

chinkara, Gazella gazella
(Pallas)?: m?gam?t?k?, 77

My private reference for that was

Prater, S. H (1993). *The book of Indian animals*. Bombay, Delhi, etc.:
Oxford University Press, fourth edn., page 268.
