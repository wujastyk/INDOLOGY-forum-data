[INDOLOGY] New publication
Dear All,
I am happy to announce the publication of "Recent Researches in Sanskrit
Computational Linguistics" edited by Malhar Kulkarni and Chaitali
Dangarikar.
This book contains selected and invited papers presented at the 5th
International Sanskrit Computational Linguistics Symposium held at IIT
Bombay, Mumbai during 4-6 January 2013.
A formal release of this book took place during a recently concluded
Conference on Sanskrit Computational Syntax at the University of Paris.
The book was released by an eminent Scientist, Gerard Huet.
DK Printworld has published this book. The notice about the book has
already been uploaded on the website of DKP which can be seen through the
following link:
    http://www.dkprintworld.com/product-detail.php?pid=1280857207
thanks.
Malhar Kulkarni.
