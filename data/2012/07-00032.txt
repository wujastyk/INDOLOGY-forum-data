The DLI has digitized Gode's *Studies in Indian Literary History* vols 1-3,
and his *Studies in Indian Cultural History* v.1.  The individual pages
have been downloaded and assembled into rational PDFs, and uploaded to
archive.org for all.  Go to http://archive.org and search for "p. k. gode
studies".  If the search index isn't updated yet, here are the direct links:

   - http://archive.org/details/StudiesInIndianLiteraryHistoryVolume1
   - http://archive.org/details/StudiesInIndianLiteraryHistoryVolume2
   - http://archive.org/details/StudiesInIndianLiteraryHistoryVolume3
   - http://archive.org/details/StudiesInIndianCulturalHistoryvolum1
