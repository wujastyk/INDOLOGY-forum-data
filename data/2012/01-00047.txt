Heidelberg Summer Schools in Spoken Sanskrit and Nepali
The Department of Classical Indology, University of Heidelberg is again organizing 
- Summer School in Spoken Sanskrit (Sadananda Das) and
- Nepali Intensive Course (Laxmi Nath Shrestha)
from 6th to 31st August, 2012 at the South Asia Institute, Heidelberg.
Applications for participation in these courses are invited
and should reach us by 15th May, 2012.
