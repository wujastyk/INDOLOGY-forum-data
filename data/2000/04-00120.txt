Tam. MUGkA/Germ. Mungo
Dravidian),
  Mongooses occur in Tolkaappiyam, said to be the earliest extant work.
  "mUGkA" is the tamil for mongoose.
   "mUGkA veruku eli mUvari aNiloTu
    AGku avai nA_nkum kuTTikku uriya" -tol.
