SOAS phone numbers
SOAS has had a new phone system installed.  All phone numbers have now
changed.  Old phone numbers still appear to ring when you call them, but
most of the old handsets in people's offices have been removed.  So if you
think you colleague isn't answering, it may be an artefact of an
imperfectly implemented technology upgrade.
The new staff phone numbers are now in the Web; the South Asia people, for
example, are at
        http://www.soas.ac.uk/SouthAsia/staff.html
