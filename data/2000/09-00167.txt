itare.saam - what does it mean?
mas. gen. pl. of itara = other.
The declension is as in tad -> te.saam, not as in raama -> raamaa.naam.
YS 1. 19 has videha-prak.rtilayaanaam.
itare.saam in 1. 20 means "of the others" i.e. not "of videhaaH or
prak.rtilayaaH".
