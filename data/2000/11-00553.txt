racial and cultural divide that has been constructed in India (both by
Indians and by Europeans ) based on the Aryan Invasion Theory.
What in the name of heaven is a three-quarter Dravidian and one-quarter Aryan
person ? Perhaps every fourth word such a person utters has impeccable I-E
etymology ?
-Arun Gupta
