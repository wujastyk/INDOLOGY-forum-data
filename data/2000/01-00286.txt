second according to other traditions) out of three verses:

sabbe saGkhArA dukkhA'ti
yadA paJJAya passati
atha nibbindati dukkhe
esa maggo visuddhiyA

sabbe saGkhArA aniccA'ti
yadA paJJAya passati
atha nibbindati dukkhe
esa maggo visuddhiyA

sabbe dhammA anattA'ti
yadA paJJAya passati
atha nibbindati dukkhe
esa maggo visuddhiyA

(here "J" stays for palatal n, "G" for the guttural one)
