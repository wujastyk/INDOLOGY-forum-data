thirumUlar thiruman-thiram:
====================
05. than-thiram
1. suththa saivam
  uurum ulakamum okkap padaikkinRa
  pEr aRivALan perumai kuRiththidin
  mEruvum mU ulaku aaLi ilangku ezun-thu
  thAraNi n-Al vakais saivamum aam ee.
2. asuththa saivam
kAthup pon aarn-tha kadukkan iraNdu sErththu
  oothum thiru mEniyuL kaddu iraNdudan
  sOthanai seythu thuvA thesa mArkkar aay
  oothi iruppAr oru saivar aakumE.
3. mArkka saivam
ponnAl siva sAthanam pUthi sAthanam
n-an mArkka sAthanam mA njAna sAthanam
thun mArkka sAthanam thOnRAtha sAthanam
san mArkka sAthanam aam suththa saivarkkE.
aakamam onpAn athil aana n-Al eezu
mEkam il n-Alu eezu muppEtham uRRu udan
vEkam il vEthAn-tha siththAn-tha meymai onRu
aaka mudin-thavarum suththa saivamE.
