The Department of Humanities at Mahidol University in Bangkok, 
Thailand, is seeking a full-time lecturer for its International PhD 
Programme in Buddhist Studies (English medium).

Candidates should have expertise in Mahayana Buddhism (especially 
Indian Mahayana) and some proficiency in Sanskrit and/or Pali. A 
knowledge of Hinduism would also be an advantage.

Candidates specializing in South-East Asian Buddhism will also be 
considered.

The position will start in October 2008.

For further details, please send an email with CV attached to Dr Justin 
Meiland at frjom at mahidol.ac.th or Dr Pathompong Bodhiprasiddhinand at 
shpbp at mahidol.ac.th.
