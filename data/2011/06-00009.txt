150th Birth Anniversary of
Gurudev Rabindranath Tagore
Rabindranath Tagore (1861 - 1941) was a Bengali poet, novelist, musician, painter and playwright.  He was the first non-European to be awarded the Nobel Prize for Literature (1913).
