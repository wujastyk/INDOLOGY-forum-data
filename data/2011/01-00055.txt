Several relevant chapters in,

*Asie IV: Enfances, sous la direction de Flora Blanchon *(Paris: Presses de
l'Universit? de Paris-Sorbonne, 1997).  ISSN 1242-5761.  ISBN 2-84050-076-0.

The first page of the table of contents is given
here<http://books.google.com/books?id=6UPIhbhdJAgC&lpg=PP1&ots=GnYBcAsOXB&dq=flora%20blanchon%20enfances&pg=PA413#v=snippet&q=table%20des%20mati%C3%A8res&f=false>;
