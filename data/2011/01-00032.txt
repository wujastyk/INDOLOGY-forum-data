10 doctoral stipends advertised at the University of Vienna for              Himilayan-related studies
*?Kulturtransfer und interkulturelle Kontakte im Grenzgebiet des Himalaya?*
(Cultural transfer and inter-cultural contact on the borderlands of the
Himalayas)
