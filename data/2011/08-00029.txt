Description

Kavya Mala Series of Books of 
Nirnaya Sagar Press, Mumbai 
published during late 19th and 
early 20th centuries. Kavya Mala 
is a collection of ancient, very 
rare and previously unpublished 
Sanskrit works. Nearly 100 books 
were published in this series.



Kavyamala Anthology Series 
(volumes 1 to 14) is uploaded at 
http://www.archive.org/details/Kavyamala_anthology_series_of_Nirnaya_sagar_press



Visit sanskritebooks.blogspot.com 
for more Sanskrit e-books.
