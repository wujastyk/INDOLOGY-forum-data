You will find an overview in Jacques Waardenburg: Classical approaches to the study of religion : aims, methods and theories of research. Vol. 2: Bibliography. Waardenburg refers to two older bibliographies: one by Winternitz (1893) and another one by Michael Johan de Goeje, in an obituary published in 1902.
