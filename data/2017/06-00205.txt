The first edition was by Lassen (I have a personal copy):
Lassen, Christianus, 1838. Anthologia sanscritica glossario instructa. In
usum scholarum edidit Christianus Lassen. Bonnae ad Rhenum: Impensis H. B.
Koenig. xv, 360 pp.
A second edition edited by Gildemeister came out in 1865:
Lassen, Christian, 1865. Anthologia sansc?itica glossario instructa, in
usum scholarum edita. (1. Aufl. Bonn 1838.) Denuo adornavit Joannes
Gildemeister. Bonn: A. Marcus. xvi, 290 pp.
It was critically reviewed by Albrecht Weber, Literarische Central-Blatt 31
(1865): 810-811. The review is reprinted in Weber's Indische Streifen II
(Berlin: Nicolaische Verlagsbuchhandlung, 1869): 292-296.
A third revised edition came out in 1868:
Lassen, Christian, 1868. Anthologia sansc?itica glossario instructa. Denuo
adornavit Ioannes Gildemeister. Editio altera novis curis retractata. Bonn:
A. Marcus. xvi, 301 pp.
