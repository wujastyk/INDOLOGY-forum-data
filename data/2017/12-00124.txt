[INDOLOGY] Price tag on 19th-cent. print
Price tags in  rUpyA/rupayyA -  aNA/aNE -  paise system have three places
for three numbers separated by hyphen.
e.g., 1 - 4 - 3 in this system means 1 rupee, 4 aNAs, 3 paise.
