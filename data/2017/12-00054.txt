Respected scholars,
Apologies for cross posting.

the following API.
http://api.sanskritworld.in/v0.0.1/jagAma/prakriya
The generic call is
http://api.sanskritworld.in/v0.0.1/<verbform>/prakriya
I would be highly obliged if the scholars can check the API and give their
remarks.
