Dear colleagues,

Here is another Sanskrit Reader, which is part of the Russian Sanskrit  
  Textbook by F.I. Knauer (1849-1917), Prof. of Saint Vladimir   
University in Kiev.
F.I. stands for Fyodor Ivanovich, the original first name was   
Friedrich. The textbook was published at the cost of the University.
Textbook of the Sanskrit Language. Grammar. Chrestomathy. Glossary.   
Leipzig: Typography of V. Drugulin, 1908.
2nd ed. Moscow: Starklayt, 2011
3rd ed. in the series Bibliotheca Sanscritica, vol.3, Moscow: ABV, 2015.
It contains the stories of Nal and Damayanti and of Savitri from the   
MBh, some hymns of the RV and the Story of the Flood from the   
Shatapatha Brahmana.
