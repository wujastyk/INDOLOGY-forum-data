Death of Ernest Bender
Ernest Bender (1919-1996), founding member of the South Asia Regional 
Studies Department of the University of Pennsylvania, also member of the 
Asian and Middle Eastern Studies Department (formerly Oriental Studies 
Dept.) from 1948-1989, and long-time editor of the Journal of the 
American Oriental Society, as well as the Society's president, passed 
away at his home in suburban Philadelphia on April 18th.
