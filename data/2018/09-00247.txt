First, a small correction in the text : it is "yasyaiva" not "yasya"

*yasyaiva* sphura?a? sad?tmakamasatkalp?rthagam bh?sate

s?k??ttattvamas?ti vedavacas? yo bodhayaty??rit?n |

yats?k??tkara??dbhavenna punar?v?ttirbhav?mbhonidhau

tasmai ?r?gurum?rtaye nama ida? ?r?dak?i??m?rtaye || 3 ||


pratyagabhinnasya is pratyak + abhinnasya = of the one that is
non-different from the pratyak.

Pratyaktattva refers to Brahman.
