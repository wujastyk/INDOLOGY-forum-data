[INDOLOGY] Professor Dr. Heinrich von Stietencron passed away
Padmashri Professor Dr. Heinrich von Stietencron, Emeritus of the Department of Indology and Comparative Religion of the Institute of Asian and Oriental Studies, University of Tuebingen, passed on January 12th at the age of 84 years.
The memorial service will take place on Friday, January 19th at 11am in the Stiftskirche at Tuebingen.
There will be an opportunity to exchange thoughts and memories afterwards.
The urn burial takes place in Ticino in the closest family circle.
