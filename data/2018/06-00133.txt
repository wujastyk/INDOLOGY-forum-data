[INDOLOGY] Mahabharata scroll online
Dear Colleagues,
You may be interested to see this new digitisation project just completed by the University of Edinburgh library team. It is a fully scrollable high resolution digital version of an 18th century scroll of the Mahabharata, with beautiful illustrations. You can see it at the following link:
https://librarylabs.ed.ac.uk/iiif/uv/?manifest=https://librarylabs.ed.ac.uk/iiif/manifest/mahabharataFinal.json
A blog post on the project giving some more details can be found here: http://libraryblogs.is.ed.ac.uk/diu/2018/06/22/a-stitch-in-time-mahabharata-delivered-online/
