marAThI bELA (jasmine)
There is indeed the Marathi word bel (Skt. bela-) referring to the
specific tree whose leaves are offered to Shiva, i.e. belapatra, and whose
fruit are supposed to have medicinal properties.  But this obviously is
different from anything like jasmine.
