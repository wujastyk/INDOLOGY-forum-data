The purpose of the Muktabodha Digital Library is:
a) To provide an overview of the Tantric/Agamic literature both from
printed editions and from unedited manuscripts.
b) To encourage the creation of critical editions of Tantric/Agamic texts
by providing on-line textual resources to scholars.

For those reasons to a large degree (though not entirely) the Muktabodha
Digital Library will now focus on creating etexts of unedited manuscripts.
Currently about 80% of the library is from out-of-copyright printed
editions and about 20% is from unedited manuscripts. The full list of
unedited manuscripts (63) in the Muktabodha Digital Library is appended to
this email.

Harry Spier
Manager, Muktabodha Digital Library
