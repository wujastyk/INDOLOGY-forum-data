[INDOLOGY] Medieval Investigations 2015 (for those interested in Medieval Nepal, 880-1769 AD)
Call for papers for a workshop on Medieval History of Nepal
Medieval Investigations 2015 is a Martin Chautari initiative to establish the significance of the medieval period (880 ? 1769 AD; 937 ? 1826 VS; 1-889 NS) in the historiography of Nepal. It has been organized to challenge, through the findings of the most recent academic research, existing perspectives on medieval history. The workshop in Kathmandu will see experts and students engaged and interested in medieval research coming together, presenting and discussing sources, methods, techniques, arguments and theories on the medieval. The papers presented at the workshop, in a revised form, will be published either under the MC book series or by other reputable publisher.
