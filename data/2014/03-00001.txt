[INDOLOGY] Letters of Moriz Winternitz (1863-1937)!
Dear Friends,For your information the
news below:Three letters written by
Moriz Winternitz to Prof. Kshetres Chandra Chattopadhyay (1896-1974; Professor
at Allahabad University, former Vice Chancellor Sampurnananda University in
Benaras) were donated to the Director Allahabad Museum Dr. Rajesh Purohit on
25.2.2014 when the museum celebrated the birth anniversary of the Austrian
Indologist. Donated by Prof. Mahesh
