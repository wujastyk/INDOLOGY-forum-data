 Dear Collegues,
Note that the sentence "the blog is furnished with RSS feeds" means that
this blog offers the possiblity to subscribe to the website RSS and thus be
alerted each a new blog is posted.
RSS means *RDF
<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Resource_Description_Framework> Site Summary*
or *Rich Site Summary* or *Really Simple Syndication*.
See: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RSS
This acronym has nothing to do with the RSS you must have in mind.
It is unfortunate, though, I must confess.
