> Mayrhofer (1992) has a root *lay-* ?sich schmiegen, anhaften?, i.e.
> ?nestle, stick to?, and cites forms like perf. *lilye*, *lay?? cakre,* aor.
> *(ny)?le??a*, and ta-participle *n?-l?na-* ?versteckt, sich an etwas
> heftend? = ?hidden, sticking to something?. Further, he compares *?le??a* to
> Hittite *?-li-i?-ta* ?hid himself?, PIE root probably **leyH-* ?to hide
> oneself?. He is less certain about connecting *pra-laya-, vi-l?yate, *suggesting
> that these forms may be built on an originally different [but possibly
> homophonous] root meaning ?cease, dwindle, fade away?.
>
> So, yes, a Dravidian origin does not seem likely.
