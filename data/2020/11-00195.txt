[INDOLOGY] Henri Saint-Simon: a colloquium (also) relevant to Indian studies
Dear All,
Please find below the links for the international colloquium "Henri
Saint-Simon (1760-1825) et le rejet de la violence" organized at the
EPHE-PSL in the form of a webinar on November 28, 2020.
The question of non-violence in Saint-Simon and his disciples is studied in
particular in comparison with non-violence in Indian culture. Apart from
the presentation by Sudhir Chandra ("Since Gandhi: Growing Anxiety about
Non-Violence"), all contributions are in French.
