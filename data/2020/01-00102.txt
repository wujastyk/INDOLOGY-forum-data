[INDOLOGY] CFP Conference on Jain Literary Practices of Transcreation, 21-22 May 2020, Ghent, Belgium
Dear members of Indology,
The Department of Indian Languages and Cultures at Ghent University is pleased to announce its conference  'Jain Practices of Literary Transcreation', held at Ghent University, Belgium on the 21st and 22nd of May 2020.
We invite scholars to present on the theme of translation, transcreation or adaptation of literary works by Jain authors. The full Call for Papers can be consulted in attachment.
Those interested in participating should send their title and abstract (max. 300 words) to  Ev.Declercq at ugent.be<mailto:Ev.Declercq at ugent.be> and heleen.dejonckheere at ugent.be<mailto:heleen.dejonckheere at ugent.be>, by the 15th of March 2020.
