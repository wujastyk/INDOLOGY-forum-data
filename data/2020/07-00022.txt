Dear list members,
I have tried to look at Sanskrit from the Indo-European perspective 
(part E is a 150 page etymological dictionary) in an as-yet unpublished 
manuscript that you can download from my webpage
https://www.wifa.uni-leipzig.de/itvwl/mikro/leute-team/prof-dr-harald-wiese.html

unfelicities, and the like.

Dictionary of Proto-Indo-Iranian".
All the best from the Junggrammatiker capital Leipzig
