Dear All,
With sadness I inform you of the passing away of Prof. Hendrik Wilhelm
(Henk) Bodewitz, last night (after his wife Dr Janneke Bodewitz passed away
end of July).
Biographical and bibliographical information about Henk Bodewitz can be
conveniently found here:
https://www.dutchstudies-satsea.nl/deelnemers/hendrik-wilhelm-henk-bodewitz/
A collection of his articles on the themes of Vedic cosmology and ethics
appeared recently at Brill Publishers, Leiden:
Vedic cosmology and ethics: selected studies. Ed. by Dory Heilijgers, Jan
Houben and Karel van Kooij. Leiden: Brill (Gonda Indological Studies 19).
Best regards,
Jan Houben
