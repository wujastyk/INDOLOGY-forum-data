Paul Hacker has an interesting analysis of iti in the ArthazAstra in  
his paper "AnvIkSikI" (included in Kleine Schriften).
He argues, if I remember correctly, that iti can convey inclusion,  
e.g. x,y,z iti AnvIkSikI means that A is included in or can be found  
in x,y and z.
