POSITION DESCRIPTION

The Department of South and Southeast Asian Studies at the University of
California, Berkeley, invites applications for the position of Lecturer in
Sanskrit. The successful applicant will teach introductory and intermediate
Sanskrit. The successful applicant will teach basic grammar as well as
reading courses in a variety of genres at the intermediate level.
Responsibilities include teaching, holding office hours, assigning grades,
preparing course materials, maintaining a course website and compiling
teaching materials and interacting with students via email, bCourses, and
Zoom conferencing software.

Department: https://sseas.berkeley.edu/
QUALIFICATIONS
Basic qualifications (required at time of application)

PhD (or equivalent international degree), or enrolled in a PhD or
equivalent international degree-granting program at the time of application.
Additional qualifications (required at time of start)

PhD (or international equivalent degree) by the start date.
Preferred qualifications

PhD (or international equivalent degree) in Sanskrit or related field by
the time of appointment.

Knowledge of Vedic Sanskrit, Prakrit, or another South Asian language
(ancient or modern) is preferred.
