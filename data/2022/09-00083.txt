as you might already know, the Journal of Hindu Studies<https://academic.oup.com/jhs/?login=false> (Oxford University Press) is looking for book-reviewers!

  3.  Jain, Kajri, Gods in the Time of Democracy<https://www.dukeupress.edu/gods-in-the-time-of-democracy>, 2021, Duke University Press (360 pages).

  4.  Moise, Ionut, Salvation in Indian Philosophy Perfection and Simplicity for Vai?e?ika<https://www.routledge.com/Salvation-in-Indian-Philosophy-Perfection-and-Simplicity-for-Vaiseika/Moise/p/book/9781032084787>, 2021, Routledge (228 pages).

  5.  O'Brien-Kop, Karen, Rethinking 'Classical Yoga' and Buddhism: Meditation, Metaphors and Materiality<https://www.bloomsbury.com/uk/rethinking-classical-yoga-and-buddhism-9781350229990/>, 2021, Bloomsbury Publishing (280 pages).

And, for a review essay:

  1.  Irani, A.A., The Muhammad Avatara: Salvation History, Translation, and the Making of Bengali Islam<https://global.oup.com/academic/product/the-muhammad-avatra-9780190089221?cc=at&lang=en&#>, 2021, Oxford University Press (456 pages).

  2.  Steward, T.K., Witness to Marvels ? Sufism and Literary Imagination<https://www.ucpress.edu/book/9780520306332/witness-to-marvels>, 2019, University of California Press (336 pages).

Should you be interested in reviewing one of the above-listed books, please send an email to me off-list at giulia.buriola at hotmail.it<mailto:giulia.buriola at hotmail.it>, briefly indicating why you are an appropriate reviewer for the book.

Do bear in mind that not all publishers are willing to provide hard copies of books for review.

Lastly, if you would like to propose a book for review, or would like to review a book that is not listed here, please do not hesitate to get in touch with me directly. We are also welcome proposals for review essays on a select grouping of related publications.


All the best,
