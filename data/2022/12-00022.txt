For the yati in K?vya, see also:
A.A.E. van der Geer, The Bh?sa Problem: a Statistical Research into its solution.
It is a study into the yati in the plays attributed to the playwright Bh?sa. The idea was that one author, in case Bh?sa, was consistent in in use of emjambement, an assumption which, however, is not borne out by the plays attributed to Har?a. Nevertheless, the study discusses a lot of material, however, as said, mainly restricted to verses in plays.
