Dear colleagues,

The South Asian Network Ghent Research group at Ghent University  (https://research.flw.ugent.be/en/sangh) invites applications for a postdoctoral fellowship (3 years) from scholars wishing to join our department. Our research group currently also hosts expertise in South Asian Buddhism, art and archeology (contact: Daniela De Simone, daniela.desimone at ugent.be), comtemporary Jainism, South Asian history and diaspora (contact: Tine Vekemans, tine.vekemans at ugent.be), South Asian language and literature (Sanskrit, Prakrit, Apabhramsha, Hindi), Jainism, and history (contact: Eva De Clercq, ev.declercq at ugent.be).

Interested scholars are invited to send us their CV and a short abstract describing their project.

The candidates will apply for a fellowship (junior or senior) through the Fund for Scientific Research ? Flanders (www.fwo.be<https://eur01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.fwo.be%2F&data=04%7C01%7CJean-Michel.Delire%40ulb.be%7C5c7a457a108a4c4d64cb08d97d35d027%7C30a5145e75bd4212bb028ff9c0ea4ae9%7C0%7C0%7C637678492938038559%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C3000&sdata=r7QHo7keCbHTSqTtP8P7Ttto77LnEtYkcxtzHXEK6Ng%3D&reserved=0>), with a deadline of December 1st.
