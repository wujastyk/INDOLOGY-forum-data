It is my pleasure to announce the 6th International Summer School in Spoken Sanskrit offered at Leipzig University from September 5 to September 30, 2022. We are currently accepting applications and invite you to forward this announcement to anyone who may be interested. The Summer School will be offered as a hybrid event.
