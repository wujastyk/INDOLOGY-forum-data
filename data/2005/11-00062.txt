Professor G.B. Palsule passed away in Pune
I just read in the internet version of the Marathi newspaper Sakal (esakal.com) that Professor
G.B. Palsule passed away in Pune on Nov 28, 2006 at the age of 85.  A memorial meeting is
to be held at the Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute in Pune on Dec 2.
