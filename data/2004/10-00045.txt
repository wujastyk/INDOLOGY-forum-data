Lexico-statistical analysis of the Avesta
Probably of interest to many readers of this
list:
An important new research tool for the study of
the Avesta has just appeared:
The Avesta: A Lexico-Statistical Analysis,
of Raymond Doctor (Acta Iranica vol. 41. Peeters
Publishers, Leuven, 105 ?uro).
It contains a direct index, reverse index, hapax
legomenon and frequency counts of words in the
Avesta.
See
http://www.peeters-leuven.be/boekoverz.asp?nr=7803
for more information.
