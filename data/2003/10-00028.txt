Sanskrit word for fusion
If you mean `metallurgical fusion', saMdhaana may be the word. See
ChU 4.17.7-8: tad-yathaa lavaNena suvarNaM saMdadhyaat suvarNena rajataM rajatena trapu .... ;
also GB 1.1.14[11.13], JUB 3.17.3 (W. Rau, Metalle und Metallgeraete im vedischen Indien, 1974,
p.30.
See also KauTilya, AZ 7.3.8-9: tejo hi saMdhaanakaaraNam, naataptaM lohaM lohena saMdhatta iti.
