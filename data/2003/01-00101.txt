New publication
Just published by University of Washington Press: Timothy Lenz, "A New Version of the Gandhari Dharmapada and a Collection of Previous-Birth Stories." This is volume 3 of "Gandharan Buddhist Texts", the publication series of the British Library/University of Washington Early Buddhist Manuscripts Project.
The book can be ordered directly from the order department of the University of Washington Press at uwpord at u.washington.edu.
