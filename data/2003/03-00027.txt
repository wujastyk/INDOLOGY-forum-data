"South Indian Scripts ..." : Corrections
Dear list members,
I have to apologize for a defective font change in my book
"South Indian Scripts ...", which I discovered only this
weekend: On pp. 174-177 of the Kannada section my printer
confused the sub-fonts for i- and e-ligatures. In other parts
of the book such as the list of Kannada "conjuncts" and the
outline table this defect does not occur.
I was aware of this problem, and to have overlooked it here is
nobody's fault but mine.
For those who already have a copy, a PDF-file with the
corrected pages is available for download/print from:
www.sub.uni-goettingen.de/ebene_1/fiindolo/temp/sis_corr.htm
Copies distributed from now on will include these corrections.
Remorsefully
