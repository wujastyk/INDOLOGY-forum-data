> are:
> 
>     ttskrit2.sit        a "stuffed" archive for the mac 
>     ttskrit2.exe        a "self-extracting .zip file" for the pc 
>     
>     (A set of HP Laserjet-compatable fonts w/ WordPerfect 5.1/6.0 printer
