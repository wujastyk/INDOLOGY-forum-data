In the seventies Hajime Nakamura published an useful 
survey of this kind:
Nakamura, Hajime: Religions and philosophies of India : a 
survey with bibliographical notes. - Tokyo : Hokuseido 
Press, 1973 (containing chap. 1 and 2, introduction and 
Brahmanism)
Chapter 3 and 4 were published in typewriter typography:
3. Third Chapter: Hinduism. - Tokyo : Hokuseido Press, 
1974
4. Fourth Chapter: Orthodox philosophical systems. - Tokyo 
: Hokuseido Press, 1973
