CS Utopia updated
Robert Zydenbos noticed a flaw in the Adobe Utopia Computer Sanskrit font
that has been available for the last year or so from the INDOLOGY web/ftp
site.
I've fixed the problem (italic "L" had an erroneous underdot), and
uploaded new versions of the files (version 1.1), both PostScript and
TrueType. 
The names are:
adobe-utopia-font-CS-encoding-PS-11.readme
adobe-utopia-font-CS-encoding-PS-11.zip
adobe-utopia-font-CS-encoding-TTF-11.readme
adobe-utopia-font-CS-encoding-TTF-11.zip
and the site for direct ftp is
  ftp.ucl.ac.uk/pub/users/ucgadkw/indology/software
