Calcutta SF
My science fiction novelette about Calcutta in the year 2070 has just
appeared in the following anthology. I managed to weave a fair number of
Sanskrit terms into the story.
_Death Looked Down._ In _New Altars: Science Fiction and Fantasy Stories
about Religion and Spirituality,_ edited by Dawn Albright and Sandra J.
Hutchinson. Arlington: Angelus Press, 1997.
ISBN: 0-9648653-6-X, $14.95, 232 pp, tp.
