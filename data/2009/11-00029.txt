NYTimes review of 2 Exhibitions of Jain Art
Two exhibitions of Jain art in New York City:
"Victorious Ones: Jain Images of Perfection" runs through Feb. 15 at the
Rubin Museum of Art, 150 West 17th Street, Manhattan
and
"Peaceful Conquerors: Jain Manuscript Painting" runs through March 28 at the
Metropolitan Museum of Art, metmuseum.org.
are reviewed in the NYTimes, including an online slideshow.
