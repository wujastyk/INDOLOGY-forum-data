>     It is with great sadness that I write to share that John C.
>     Huntington passed away this past Sunday, November 28th, after an
>     extended illness. He is survived by his wife and partner in
>     art-historical studies, Susan, their son Eric, and
>     daughter-in-law, Yumi.
>
>     John was a supportive adviser and mentor, who nurtured many
>     students in their pursuit of knowledge about Buddhist and
>     Himalayan art during his tenure at Ohio State. His publications on
>     Buddhist art spanned the vast and diverse regions of Asia, but
>     focused primarily on South Asia and the Himalayas. The John C. and
>     Susan L. Huntington Photographic Archive of Buddhist and Asian
>     Art, originally at The Ohio State University and now at the
>     University of Chicago, stands as a lasting legacy of John's
>     scholarship and photographic documentation of art and architecture
>     across Asia. John?s absence is already deeply felt.
