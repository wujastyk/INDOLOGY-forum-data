[INDOLOGY] Soma substitute in 1975 Agnicayana
AGNI I, p. 186:
"The Nambudiris have evolved an arrangement that provides them with
a substitute for Soma. This substitute, probably Sarcostemma brevistigma,
an Asclepiad related to the American milkweeds, is also used in other parts of India."
