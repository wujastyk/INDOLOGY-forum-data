Northern Illinois University, DeKalb (NIU), University of Illinois at  
Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) and International Ramayana Institute of North  
America (IRINA) are holding the Third International Ramayana  
Conference at the DeKalb campus of NIU in the United States on  
September 18-19, 2010. Scholarly papers are invited for the conference.
We look forward to receiving the paper before Monday, May 17, 2010.
For details on the conference, registration, and abstract submission,  
visit: www.cseas.niu.edu/CSEAS/conferences/Ramayana/
Kindly pass this information on to your colleagues who may be  
interested in this conference.
