Lost manuscript of Hanxleden's Sanskrit grammar rediscovered
Dear colleagues,
For the ones interested in the XVIIIth century Indology, I have the 
pleasure to announce that the lost manuscript of Johann Ernst 
Hanxleden/Arnos Padiri's Sanskrit grammar has been found in an 
Italian monastery. More about at:
