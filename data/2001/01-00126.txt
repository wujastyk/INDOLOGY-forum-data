Bengali sAyi ?
There is no Bengali word 'gosAyi'; the proper Bengali word for Skt. gosvAmI
is 'gosAi~'[the final syllable being-short i and candravindu] or in old
orthography 'gosAin~'[the final syllable is the nasal of the palatal group].
