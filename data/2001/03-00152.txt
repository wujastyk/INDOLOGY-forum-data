Electronic Mahabharata: supplementary passages released (fwd)
The project to create a complete, accurate electronic text of the
Mahabharata has now reached completion, with the release of the
supplementary passages (star passages and Appendix I passages). Like
the main text, the supplementary material can be downloaded by
visiting the following website:
http://bombay.oriental.cam.ac.uk/john/mahabharata/statement.html
