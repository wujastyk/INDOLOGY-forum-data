sULe in Kannada
I'm by no means a lexicographer, but sULe as I understand it means
prostitute.  A common insult in kannaDa is sULe-magane lit. son of a
prostitute (colloquially used with the effect similar to 'son of a
bitch').  If you go to Hampi, you can even see the sULe bAzAr!
