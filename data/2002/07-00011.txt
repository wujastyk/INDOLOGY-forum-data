Dear Timothy Lubin,

For the second part of your second quotation cf. Paa"supatasuutra
5.35--38:

chittvaa do.saa.naa.m hetujaalasya muulam (5.35)
buddhyaa (5.36)
sa.mcittam (5.37)
sthaapayitvaa ca rudre (5.38)

The complete verse also occurs in Li"ngapuraa.na 2.18.40.
