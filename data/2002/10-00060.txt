New RSLP project: Mapping Asia
For info:
Mapping Asia is a tool to improve access to UK library holdings for
researchers working in all subject areas of the humanities and social
sciences relating to Asia, the Middle East and North Africa. Mapping Asia
provides:
* Collection descriptions of resources held in university, special and
public libraries
* Access to holdings of newspapers in any language published in Asia, the
Middle East and North Africa
* Information on the range of linguistic expertise in Asian languages
available across the UK
        Full details of the service can be found at
http://www.asiamap.ac.uk
